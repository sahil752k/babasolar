Frequently Asked questions ?

Find answers to common questions about solar energy and our services

Most homeowners save 70-90% on their electricity bills after installing solar panels. The exact savings depend on your energy consumption, roof size, local sunlight conditions, and the efficiency of the solar system. On average, a 3kW system can save you ‚Çπ18,000-‚Çπ25,000 annually.

A typical residential solar installation takes 3-7 days, depending on the system size and complexity. This includes site assessment, mounting structure installation, panel placement, electrical work, and connection to the grid. We ensure minimal disruption to your daily routine during installation.

Standard grid-tied systems shut down during power outages for safety reasons. However, systems with battery backup or hybrid inverters can continue to power your essential appliances during outages. We can design a system that meets your specific backup power requirements.

Solar panels require minimal maintenance. Occasional cleaning to remove dust and debris is recommended, especially in dry climates. Most systems come with monitoring software to track performance. We offer annual maintenance packages to ensure optimal system performance throughout its lifespan.
